Secured living in a rapidly developing environment



Sadashivpet is a suburb of immense potential, it holds the unparalleled advantage of location. It is fast emerging as the next big investment destination of Hyderabad with smart and amplifying infrastructure in place. It is very pleasant suburb to live and has excellent access of the four-lane road to reach ORR (Outer Ring Road) as well as Hyderabad via Patancheru and Kukatpally and just a few kilometers away from Woxsen Business school as well as NIMZ (National Investment for Manufacturing Zones) near Zaheerabad, which is a 13000 acres mega manufacturing zone proposed by Central Government of India which was creating nearly 5 Lakes employment. It is pride of Telangana.
The excellent transport connectivity, well-developed roads and civic facilities have coerced the buyers to invest in this area. Strategically located in this tranquil and flourishing neighbourhood with excellent connectivity to the twin cities is Brundavanam, a magnificent 100 acre project with outstanding features that are required for your weekend getaway.
