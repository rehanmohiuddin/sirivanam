About Brindavanam

Walk inside the majestic arch of Brundavanam and a whole new world awaits you. Discover the lush, green lands - a verdant expanse of 100 acres of unpolluted goodness. Located near Sadashivpet, Brundavanam is a pristine venture of peace and tranquillity, a dream only a few can afford. This organic farmland is a secure gated community with extensive open plots. Each plot includes the option to grow the choicest of trees and to build your own customizable villa. An impeccable land like this, with all modern facilities and amenities, away from the bustling polluted cityscape and yet having exceptional connectivity, makes it a sensible investment for tomorrow that offers guaranteed returns in the future. Brundavanam is an ideal place for tranquil living and the best place for your children to grow with nature.
